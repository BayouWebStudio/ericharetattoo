Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Eric located?

Eric is based in San Diego, CA — working out of Guru Tattoo Little Italy and The Tattoo Temple.

Does Eric do walk-ins?

Eric primarily works by appointment to ensure every design receives the time and attention it deserves. Please book in advance via CoCreate Ink.

How far in advance should I book?

Availability varies — check current openings through the booking portal. For larger pieces, booking several weeks ahead is recommended.

Does Eric design custom pieces?

Yes — every piece Eric creates is fully custom. He does not work from flash or repeat designs. Your tattoo is unique to you.

What styles does Eric specialize in?

Sacred geometry, mandalas, dotwork, geometric sleeves, chest pieces, and precision line work. All work is black ink.
