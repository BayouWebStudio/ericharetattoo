Biography

Geometry as a Spiritual Practice

Eric Hare is a geometric tattoo artist based in San Diego, CA, specializing in sacred geometry, mandalas, dotwork, and precision line work. His journey into tattooing was shaped by a deep study of Buddhism and mindfulness — a transformative path that infused his work with spiritual intention and geometric precision.

Every piece Eric creates lives on the boundary between mathematics and art. His work spans full sleeves to intricate chest pieces — each design a custom exploration of symmetry, dotwork, and organic pattern. Whether you're drawn to strict geometric structures or flowing mandalas, Eric collaborates closely to bring your vision to life.
